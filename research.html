Generalized homogeneous derivations in graded rings
Preprint - Under Review
üìë ArXiv üì• PDF
üìñ View Abstract
We introduce generalized homogeneous derivations on graded rings, extending the homogeneous derivations introduced by Kanunnikov. We define gr-generalized derivations that preserve the graded structure and extend several results from prime rings to gr-prime rings. We also characterize when gr-semiprime rings contain non-trivial central graded ideals and examine the algebraic properties of these derivations. Finally, we construct functorial properties and categorical frameworks for derivation structures in both ring and module settings.
Differential Structures in Graded Rings and Modules
Under review
üìù Draft
üìñ View Abstract
In this paper, we study homogeneous derivations which preserve the degree and we will call them dp-derivations. We establish fundamental structural properties, proving that inverse images of gr-prime differential ideals are integrally closed in commutative graded rings with dp-derivations. For differential simplicity, we show that gr-simple rings of prime characteristic have gr-nilradical as the unique gr-prime ideal. We demonstrate that dp-skew polynomial rings preserve the gr-Noetherian property, extending the Hilbert basis theorem to differential graded contexts.
