Current Focus

My current research investigates the interplay between algebraic geometry and number theory, seeking to uncover the fundamental patterns that connect these disciplines. I am particularly interested in how mathematical structures reflect the inherent order found in nature.

Through my work, I aim to contribute to our understanding of the mathematical foundations that describe our universe, drawing inspiration from the natural harmony and balance observed in the world around us.
